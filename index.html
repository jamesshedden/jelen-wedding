Welcome to our wedding website! You should find everything you need to know here, but if there’s anything you’re not sure about, just get in touch.

Venue

We’re getting married in a deconsecrated church in Hampstead called St Stephen’s, Rossyln Hill and we’ll be having our reception at the same venue.

You can find more information on public transport options on the venue’s website. There’s no parking at the venue, but there should be some on-street parking available nearby — although we can’t guarantee it’ll be unoccupied so if you’re not sure it may be best to use public transport.

On the day

The ceremony starts at 2pm, so please arrive in good time.

We’ll then be having drinks & canapés before dinner & speeches, followed by cake, dancing, tea & coffee and evening snacks, with celebrations winding up around 11pm.

Dress code

We’d love to see you dressed up for the special occasion, but we’ll be happy with whatever makes you comfortable. We might be outside briefly for confetti, so pack an extra layer as it’s likely to be chilly!

Accommodation

If you’re visiting from outside London and need somewhere to stay, there’s a Premier Inn very conveniently located nearby. If you’d like other recommendations, let us know and we’ll be happy to help.

Gifts

We know attending a wedding costs time & money, so don’t feel like you have to get us anything!

If you would like to give something, we’re using Prezola to take contributions for our honeymoon fund. We’ve been living together for a long time and have most things we need, so what we’d really love is an adventure to mark the start of our married life — any money will go towards making our honeymoon a memorable one.
